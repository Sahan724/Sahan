Welcome to Horizon Campus

We offer UGC approved 15 accredited degree courses under the five faculties, Faculty of Education, Faculty of Information Technology, Faculty of International Programs, Faculty of Management and Faculty of Science. Every year, more than 1200 students get enrolled in the academic programmes for Sri Lankan degrees as well as the degrees offered by top universities in UK, USA, and Asia.

We Offer

Horizon Campus offers a diverse range of accredited programs across five UGC-approved faculties, modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a student-focused environment that fosters academic and personal growth.

15 + Degree Programs

Horizon Campus offers over 15 diverse degree programs, providing students with a wide range of academic options. Each program is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for success in their chosen fields.

Expert Lecturers

Horizon Campus boasts a team of expert lecturers who bring extensive industry experience and academic knowledge to the classroom, ensuring students receive high-quality education and practical insights.

Online Education

Horizon Campus offers flexible online education, allowing students to access quality degree programs remotely. These online courses provide the same rigorous curriculum and support as on-campus programs, making education accessible from anywhere.

Our Faculties

Horizon Campus hosts five UGC-approved faculties, each offering accredited programs and staffed by experienced educators. These faculties provide specialized resources and meet national standards, ensuring students receive high-quality education in their chosen fields.

Our Facilities

Horizon Campus features modern lecture halls, state-of-the-art labs, a well-stocked library, computer labs, and recreational facilities, including a gym and sports areas. The campus also offers Wi-Fi access and student lounges for relaxation.

Library Facilities

Horizon Campus features well-equipped library facilities, offering a vast collection of academic resources, including books, journals, and digital materials. The library provides a quiet, conducive environment for study and research, supporting students in their academic pursuits.

New Volleyball Court

Horizon Campus features a new volleyball court, offering students a modern facility for sports and recreational activities, enhancing campus life and promoting physical fitness.

New Cricket Ground

Horizon Campus boasts a new cricket ground, enhancing its sports facilities and providing students with an excellent venue for both practice and competitive play.

Cafeteria Facilities

Horizon Campus features a cafeteria with modern facilities, offering a variety of food and beverage options. It's a comfortable space for students to relax, socialize, and enjoy meals between classes.

Computer Labs

Horizon Campus is equipped with advanced computer labs that provide students with access to the latest technology and software. These labs support hands-on learning and research, ensuring students gain practical experience in a tech-driven environment.

Swimming Pool

Horizon Campus features a well-maintained swimming pool, providing students with a space for relaxation, fitness, and recreational activities, enhancing the overall campus experience.
