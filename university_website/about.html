About Us

About Horizon

Horizon Campus is a leading private university in Sri Lanka geared to provide an inspiring learning experience to students within a holistic university atmosphere. The Campus opened its doors marking a cornerstone in the national educational sector of the country, with the aim of delivering a fully-fledged generation to the world of tomorrow. Horizon Campus offers University Grants Commission recognised 32 accredited degree courses under the six faculties, Faculty of Education, Faculty of Information Technology, Faculty of International Programs, Faculty of Law, Faculty of Technology, Faculty of Management and Faculty of Science. Every year, more than 1200 students get enrolled in the academic programmes for Sri Lankan degrees as well as the degrees offered by top universities in UK, USA, and Asia. We are proud to be approved by the Non-state sector of Ministry of Higher Education, Association of CommonWealth, Asia Pacific Quality Network and University of London. The TRIPLE ACCREDITED status of Horizon Campus and other recognition, guarantees you of a nationally and internationally recognized qualification.

Strategic Goals

“Strategic goals help to define the direction in which the Campus must travel, and assists in establishing realistic objectives that are in line with the vision and mission statements of the Campus. They offer both the much-needed foundation from which the Campus can grow, and also help establishing the roles and boundaries for every person, thus improving efficient decision making and creating a greater sense of overall momentum and direction.”
The Strategic Goals of Horizon Campus are,

A. Achieve Excellence in Tertiary Education and Learning
B. Achieve Excellence in Research and Development
C. Strengthen Human Capital and Ensure Good Governance
D. Ensure State-of-the-Art Infrastructure
E. Ensure Sound Financial Management
F. Safeguard Student Wellbeing
G. Fulfil Social Responsibility, Contribute to National Development and Ensure Global Visibility

Partners & Achievements

The Horizon Campus achieved the rare status of approval from the Ministry of Higher Education and the University Grants Commission (UGC) in Sri Lanka to award its own degrees. Horizon Campus is also the only fully private institution to be listed on the prestigious Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU) and a “Registered Centre” to offer University of London Degree programmes in Sri Lanka.

The Horizon Campus has become an Institutional Member of the Asia Pacific Quality Network, a renowned global institute for maintaining quality in higher education institutes. It has entered in to a memorandum of understanding with the Arkansas State University, USA, and Nilai University, Malaysia to offer degree programs in Biotechnology.
